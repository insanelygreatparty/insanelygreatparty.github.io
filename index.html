The Insanely Great Party – An organization of people that wish to serve Honestly, Competently, and with the full Commitment of our lives

The Insanely Great Party is a semi-political party and organization dedicated to providing the most honest, competent, and committed leaders modern humanity can produce. The party has a platform, but this platform neither conflicts nor condones any of the Republican, Liberal, Green, Libertarian, or other political parties. The IG-Party is about principles of great service that can be truly present or quite lacking in political party candidates and their platforms. The IG-Party is about being exceptional leaders: honest, competent, and committed at the least, and from that strong platform of true service, being even greater than we could each imagine. Or maybe simply as great as we can imagine.
